About Our Project

Our mission is to bridge sustainable farming practices with modern AI technology. This platform enables farmers to monitor crop health, receive real-time suggestions, and optimize water and nutrient use through intelligent predictions.

Built by a passionate team of developers and agricultural enthusiasts, our system integrates IoT sensor data with machine learning models to automate outlier detection and decision-making.

We aim to empower both small-scale and commercial farmers by providing an accessible, data-driven dashboard that supports better crop yield, efficient resource use, and eco-friendly farming.
